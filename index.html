Why Vyro

Vyro is a marketplace connecting brands with approximately 80,000 vetted short-form video creators across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube Shorts. Our creators distribute content in a way that feels native and organic to each platform,not promotional. We operate on a CPM model: you set a rate, and you only pay for views actually delivered.

Every creator in our network is pre-vetted through OAuth backend analytics access. We check viewership geography, engagement quality, and suspicious engagement patterns to ensure real reach with real audiences.

🔍

FTC Compliance

03 Campaign Strategy

Timeline & Execution

A concentrated two-week campaign window leading up to the May 22, 2026 theatrical release. Maximum intensity, maximum saturation.

May 8 – 22, 2026
Launch Wave
Flood social platforms with clipping content. Establish presence across all major short-form platforms.
May 17 – 22, 2026
White Glove & UGC Activation
White Glove premium page placements and UGC stunts go live to build anticipation as the theatrical release approaches.

Asset Requirements

  • ▶More assets equals better performance. Platform algorithms prioritize new content, so a diverse asset library is essential for sustained reach.
  • ▶We recommend approximately 15 assets to start.
  • ▶For reference: the Send Help campaign leveraged red carpet and premiere footage that drove strong engagement, leaning into fandom and recognizable celebrities as the focus.
🎬

Trailers & TV Spots

Official trailers, teasers, and TV spots, the foundation of the clipping library.

🎥

Behind-the-Scenes

BTS footage, cast interviews, and press tour moments that feel intimate and shareable.

🌟

Red Carpet & Premiere

Red carpet and premiere content, drives strong engagement from fandom and celebrity-style pages.

Call-to-Action Strategy

While clipping is a top-of-funnel awareness strategy, we can still require a CTA. Provide a ticket tracking link and require creators to include it in the caption or pin comment on every piece of content.

In the past, many theatrical release campaigns required creators to include the movie title + "in theaters" + release date, either on screen or in the caption. For example: "The Mandalorian & Grogu in theaters, May 22, 2026"

05 Our Recommendation

Our Recommendation

Our recommendation is to launch the campaign two weeks before the theatrical release date. For The Mandalorian & Grogu (May 22, 2026), that means going live on May 8, 2026. This gives the clipping engine enough time to build momentum, with content volume peaking as opening weekend approaches. All we need to get started is a library of approved assets, at least 15 clips (trailers, BTS footage, premiere content, TV spots). More assets means more variety across creators, which means better performance from platform algorithms.
